# What is CreateFlow?
*A Guide for Creators & Students*

## Introduction
CreateFlow is your personal "AI Production Studio." Imagine having a full team of photographers, videographers, and directors inside your computer, ready to bring your ideas to life instantly.

Instead of needing expensive cameras, actors, or travel budgets, CreateFlow lets you generate professional social media content—images, videos, and entire mini-series—using advanced Artificial Intelligence.

---

## The 4 Creative Studios
CreateFlow is divided into four main "Studios," each designed for a specific step in the content creation process.

### 1. The Workflow Wizard (Quick Mode)
**Best for:** Rapidly creating character portraits and testing new looks.
Think of this as your "Photo Booth." You simply pick:
*   **Who?** (Select a Character)
*   **What are they wearing?** (Select an Outfit)
*   **Where are they?** (Select a Vibe/Location)

The AI thinks for a moment and snaps a photo. It’s the fastest way to get content.

### 2. The World Builder (Director Mode)
**Best for:** Telling a bigger story with friends, props, and specific details.
Think of this as your "Movie Set." Here, you have total control:
*   **Cast:** Add friends, pets, or background actors.
*   **Props:** Hand your character a coffee cup, a laptop, or put them in a specific car.
*   **The Director's Chair:** Use the "Storyboard" feature to plan out 4 specific shots in a row (e.g., "Walking in," "Ordering coffee," "Sitting down," "Laughing").

### 3. The Video Studio (Motion)
**Best for:** Turning your still images into viral videos.
Once you have a perfect image, bring it here to add movement. You can:
*   **Animate:** Make the character wave, walk, or smile.
*   **Camera Control:** Pan the camera like a drone or zoom in for a dramatic close-up.
*   **Motion Transfer:** Upload a video of yourself dancing, and the AI character will mimic your exact moves!

### 4. Mini Series Studio (Showrunner Mode)
**Best for:** Creating your own AI TV show or social media series.
This is the most powerful tool. You act as the "Showrunner":
*   **Create a Bible:** Define your show's genre (e.g., Romance, Thriller) and tone.
*   **Write the Script:** You provide a simple idea (e.g., "The main character goes on a bad date"), and the AI writes a full script.
*   **Produce:** Click one button, and the system generates every single shot for the episode automatically, keeping the characters and outfits consistent.

---

## Key Terms to Know
*   **Prompt:** The instructions you give the AI. CreateFlow writes most of these for you, but you can always add your own "Creative Direction."
*   **Assets:** The digital files for your Characters, Outfits, and Locations.
*   **Campaign Queue:** A "To-Do List" for the AI. You can queue up 50 different image ideas and let the computer generate them overnight while you sleep.

## Your Private Workspace
Everything you create in CreateFlow is saved to your private folder on your computer. 
*   No one else sees your work until you choose to post it.
*   All your videos and images are organized by "Show" or "Campaign" so you never lose track of your files.
